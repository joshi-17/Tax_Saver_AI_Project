1. **High EMI vs Low Rent Gap**
   - When EMI is 2.5x+ of rent, renting usually wins
   - You invest the difference at 10% return

2. **High Interest Rates**
   - Above 9.5%, interest costs become prohibitive
   - Tax benefit cap at â‚¹2L limits savings

3. **High Property Prices**
   - â‚¹1Cr+ properties with 90% loans
   - EMI becomes very high

4. **Low Rent Deals**
   - Finding rent below typical market rate
   - Maximizes investment opportunity

5. **Low Property Appreciation**
   - If appreciation < 6%, renting often better
   - Investment returns beat property gains
